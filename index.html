Everything you need to know about the Llizar platform

Llizar is designed for building and deploying interactive digital experiences at scale. This includes real-time applications, multiplayer systems, content delivery platforms, data-intensive dashboards, and any application requiring low-latency, high-availability infrastructure. Our SDK supports multiple languages and frameworks, giving your team the flexibility to work with their preferred tools.

Our auto-scaling infrastructure monitors your application metrics in real-time and provisions additional compute resources within seconds. You define minimum and maximum thresholds, and Llizar handles the rest—including load balancing, connection draining, and graceful scaling. There is no manual intervention required, and you only pay for the resources you consume.

Llizar maintains SOC 2 Type II certification and is compliant with GDPR and CCPA requirements. Our infrastructure undergoes regular penetration testing by independent third parties. We provide data residency controls, encryption at rest and in transit (AES-256 / TLS 1.3), and comprehensive audit logging. Enterprise customers can request our full security documentation and latest audit reports.

Llizar uses transparent, usage-based pricing with no hidden fees. You pay for the compute, bandwidth, and storage you consume. All plans include our full feature set—no feature gating. Enterprise customers with annual commitments receive volume discounts of up to 35%. Contact our sales team for a custom quote tailored to your projected usage.

Enterprise plans include a dedicated solutions engineer, 24/7 priority support with 15-minute response times for critical issues, quarterly architecture reviews, and access to our private Slack channel with the Llizar engineering team. Llizar operates infrastructure across North America (US-East, US-West, Canada), Europe (Frankfurt, London, Amsterdam), and Asia-Pacific (Singapore, Tokyo, Sydney). Enterprise customers can specify data residency requirements to ensure compliance with local regulations. Additional regions are available upon request for large-scale deployments.
